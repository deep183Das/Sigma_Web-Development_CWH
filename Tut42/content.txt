CSS Transforms -> two types
    ~> 2D Transforms
        This property allows the content to be restructured in ways like translate,
        rotate, scale or skew etc. As it is 2D, therefore the content si Transformed
        into x and y dimensions.
    ~> 3D Transforms
        We can manipulate the objects in 3D (in addition to z axis)
