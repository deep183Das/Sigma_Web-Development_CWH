
id (an unique identifier)
    An ID is an "attribute", a unique identifier assigned to only one HTML element within a page.
    It is often used for unique styling and JavaScript manipulations.

    It is also used for generating links to the specific using # after the page's link, then the page will will be 
    opened with the part which has that specific id.
    
class
    The class "attribute" lets you give the same name to multipe HTML elements. That way, you can easily change
    their look or behaviour all at once. Classes are not unique and can be assigned to multiple elements. They 
    are genrally used for applying the same styles or behaviour to a group of elements.
